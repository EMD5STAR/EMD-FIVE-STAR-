About

Ethen Dewa Moyo, born on February 22, 2005, and known by his stage name EMD FIVE-STAR, is a rising music artist from Johannesburg, South Africa. With his soulful and lovely sound, he’s quickly becoming an artist to watch. Widely known as "The Five-Star Boy," EMD FIVE-STAR is mysterious and shy in person, yet confident and expressive in his music — creating emotional, melodic tunes that resonate across South Africa.
